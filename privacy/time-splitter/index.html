TimeSplitter Privacy Policy

Effective date: 15 July 2026

TimeSplitter is provided by NexBulb Labs. This policy explains how data is handled when you use the Android app.

Your activity data

Your categories, sessions, timer history, notes, goals, settings, exports, and backups are stored locally on your device. NexBulb Labs does not receive this activity data. Export and backup files are created only when you choose a location through Android's system file picker.

Advertising and consent

TimeSplitter uses Google Mobile Ads to display a limited banner and Google's User Messaging Platform to collect or manage privacy choices where required. Depending on your region, settings, and consent choice, Google and its partners may collect or share IP-address-derived approximate location, app and ad interactions, diagnostic information, and device or other account identifiers. These data may be used for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention. Data sent by these Google services is encrypted in transit.

Where required, you can review or change your choices from TimeSplitter's Settings screen by selecting “Privacy choices”. Personalized advertising is used only where permitted by the applicable consent signal; otherwise Google may provide limited or non-personalized advertising.

Permissions and foreground timer

TimeSplitter may request notification permission and uses a foreground service while a timer is running or paused. The visible notification keeps the activity timer working in the background and provides Pause, Resume, and Stop controls. It is not used to collect location or send your activity history to NexBulb Labs.

Data deletion

You can delete individual sessions and categories in the app or erase all local app data from Settings -> Data Management. Uninstalling TimeSplitter also removes app data retained by Android, subject to your device backup settings and any files you exported separately.

Children

TimeSplitter is intended for users aged 13 and over and is not designed for families or directed to children under 13.

Third-party policy

Google's handling of data is governed by Google's own privacy policies and advertising controls.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact nexbulblabs@gmail.com.
