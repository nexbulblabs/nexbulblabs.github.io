Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026
Last updated: July 13, 2026

Revision App is designed to work primarily on your device. NexBulb Labs does not collect, sell, rent or share your personal data through the app.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Revision App, developed and published by NexBulb Labs, handles information when you use the application.

By using Revision App, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information stored by the app

Revision App allows you to create and manage information such as:

  • Subjects and topics
  • Study and revision notes
  • Revision dates and schedules
  • Completion and progress information
  • Confidence levels and learning status
  • Reminder preferences
  • Application settings
  • Backup data created by you

This information is stored locally on your device. NexBulb Labs does not receive or remotely access this information.

3. Personal information

Revision App does not require you to create an account or provide your name, phone number, address or other personal identification information.

The app does not automatically transmit your revision records, notes or study activity to NexBulb Labs.

4. Backup, export and restore

Revision App may allow you to export your app data to a backup file and restore data from a file selected by you.

Backup and export files are created only when you initiate the relevant action. You control where these files are stored, transferred or shared.

NexBulb Labs does not automatically receive copies of your backup, import or export files.

5. Notifications

Revision App may use local device notifications to remind you about scheduled revisions or study activities.

Notification scheduling and delivery are handled on your device. Reminder information is not sent to NexBulb Labs.

You can disable notifications through the app settings or your device's system settings.

6. Permissions

Depending on your device and Android version, Revision App may request permissions necessary for features such as:

  • Displaying notifications
  • Selecting a backup file for import
  • Saving an exported or backup file

Permissions are used only to provide the feature for which they are requested.

7. Advertising and analytics

Revision App does not currently include third-party advertising, user-tracking analytics or behavioural profiling services.

NexBulb Labs does not sell user information to advertisers or data brokers.

8. Feedback and support emails

Revision App may provide an option to contact NexBulb Labs using your chosen email application.

When you voluntarily send an email, NexBulb Labs receives the information you include in that message. A support email may also contain technical information that is displayed to you before sending, such as:

  • The Revision App version
  • Your Android version
  • Your device model

This information is not sent automatically. Your email provider's own privacy policy also applies when you send an email.

9. Data sharing

NexBulb Labs does not collect or share your locally stored revision information with third parties.

You may independently choose to share exported files through applications or services installed on your device. Such sharing is controlled by you and is subject to the privacy practices of the selected third-party service.

10. Data retention and deletion

Your revision information remains on your device until you edit it, delete it, clear the app's storage or uninstall the app.

Backup and exported files remain in the location chosen by you until you delete them.

Because Revision App does not currently create online user accounts or store your revision records on NexBulb Labs servers, there is no server-side account or revision database that must be deleted by NexBulb Labs.

11. Data security

Revision App is designed to minimise data collection by keeping revision information locally on your device.

You are responsible for protecting access to your device and any exported backup files. No electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

12. Children's privacy

Revision App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app stores user-created revision information locally and does not require an online account.

A parent or guardian who believes that a child has directly provided personal information to NexBulb Labs may contact us using the address below.

13. Changes to this policy

NexBulb Labs may update this Privacy Policy when Revision App's features, data practices or legal requirements change.

Updates will be published on this page, and the “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.
